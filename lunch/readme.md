# Pesto Pasta with Lemon, Spinach, Edamame & Toasted Almonds

## Description

<!-- 1-2 sentences about the dish. Where does it come from? What makes it special? -->

### Ingredients:
<!-- List every ingredient -->
- 8 ounces spaghetti
- 1/2 cup pesto, store-bought or homemade
- 8 ounces spinach
- 2 cups edamame (shelled and shucked; the peas, not entire pods)
- Juice from 2 lemons (plus fresh lemon wedges for serving)
- 3/4 cup almonds, crushed and lightly toasted add ingredient here
      
### Steps for preparation:
<!-- Each step should be a complete action. Assume the reader has never made this before. -->
1. Heat a large pot of water to boiling, and cook pasta until al dente.
2. Strain and rinse with cold water.
3. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, pesto, and spinach, and stir until evenly mixed (some spinach will wilt, some will stay firm â€” this is a nice contrast of textures).
4. Stir in the edamame.
5. Squeeze the lemon all over the finished dish.
6. Reserve a few lemon slices for people to add more if they like.
7. On a low heat, toast crushed almonds until just fragrant. Garnish pasta with the toasted almonds.
